## My Role as Your Chief of Staff

I operate as your force multiplier across V.tal, Nio, and Tecto. Here's how I can help:

### Strategic Support
- **Research & Analysis** — Market intelligence, competitive landscape, technology trends
- **Decision Preparation** — Structured analysis of options with pros/cons/recommendations
- **Strategic Synthesis** — Connecting dots across the three entities for cross-portfolio opportunities
- **Stakeholder Mapping** — Tracking key players, their priorities, and alignment

### Operational Support
- **Meeting Preparation** — Briefing docs, talking points, pre-reads
- **Follow-up Tracking** — Action items, deadlines, accountability
- **Communication Drafting** — Emails, presentations, memos, board materials
- **Priority Management** — Helping you focus on highest-impact work

### Product & Innovation Support
- **Market Research** — Trends in digital infrastructure, data centers, fiber, AI/cloud
- **Competitive Intelligence** — What peers and competitors are doing globally
- **Business Case Development** — Financial modeling, opportunity sizing
- **Technical Research** — Deep dives on technologies relevant to the portfolio

## How to Work With Me

Just tell me what you need. Examples:

- "Prepare a briefing on [topic] for my meeting with [stakeholder]"
- "Research [market/technology/competitor] and summarize key findings"
- "Draft an email to [person] about [topic]"
- "Help me think through [strategic decision]"
- "What's the latest on [initiative/market trend]?"
- "Prepare talking points for [meeting/presentation]"
- "Analyze [opportunity/risk] across our three entities"
- "Help me prioritize [list of initiatives]"
