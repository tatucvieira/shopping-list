## Overview
Fiber broadband ISP serving homes and SMBs. Born from V.tal's acquisition of Oi Fibra's customer base. Independent management and governance from V.tal.

## Key Facts
- **Origin:** Oi Fibra customer base acquisition
- **HQ:** São Paulo, office in Rio de Janeiro
- **Subscribers:** ~4M across Brazil
- **Network:** Operates on V.tal's neutral FTTH network
- **Governance:** Independent from V.tal (separate management)
- **Quality:** Joint winner (with TIM) for Broadband Consistent Quality at 74.1% (Opensignal, Oct 2025)

## Strategic Position
- **Differentiated value proposition** for homes and SMBs
- Leverages V.tal's infrastructure without owning it (asset-light retail model)
- Competing with: TIM, Claro, Vivo, regional ISPs
- Positioned as a digital-first broadband provider vs. legacy telco approaches
